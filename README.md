🚀 Featured Projects

RallyNav is a system that helps you navigate. It is like having a co-driver from rally racing in your car. In rally racing the co-driver looks at the track. Tells the driver about the turns that are coming up and how sharp they are. This way the driver knows what to expect. Can get ready especially when it is hard to see or the road is very curvy. The people who made RallyNav thought why not use this idea for driving? So of having a person in the car with you the system looks at the road and tells you when to turn and how sharp the turn is. It says things like "Right 6" or "Left 3" which means you will be turning right or left and how sharp the turn is. This helps you know what is coming up. You can get ready. There is a website that you can use to try out RallyNav. You can put in where you want to go. It will show you all the turns on the way. It is, like having your co-driver but it is a computer system instead
